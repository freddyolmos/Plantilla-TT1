# TT1 Content Checklists

## Chapter 1 (Inicial)
- Problem statement with measurable impact.
- Justification in educational and mechatronic dimensions.
- General and specific objectives (TT1 vs TT2).
- Antecedentes table with comparable systems and citations.

## Chapter 2 (Media)
- Methodological section tied to project decisions.
- Needs, requirements, and constraints with measurable values.
- Functional and physical architecture.
- Design selection criteria and validation plan.

## Chapter 3 (Final)
- Conclusions tied to objectives.
- Future work tied to clear technical next steps.

## Quality gates
- Each requirement has at least one verification method.
- Each figure/table has label, caption, and mention in text.
- Every citation key resolves in `Bibliografia.bib`.
